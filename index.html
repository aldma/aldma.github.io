Bio

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Stanford University, where I am advised by Professor Stephen Boyd. I conduct research in convex optimization and machine learning, and I'm passionate about building foundational tools for both. I am a principal developer of CVXPY, a domain-specific language for convex optimization that is used by many universities and companies. Previously, I was a full-time software engineer on the Google Brain team, where I worked on TensorFlow 2 and the core TensorFlow runtime.
