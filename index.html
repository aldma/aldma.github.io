About me

I am a Research Assistant and Ph.D. Candidate in the Aerospace Engineering Department at UniBw, under the supervision of Prof. Matthias Gerdts in the Engineering Mathematics group. Before moving to Munich, I received a B.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineering and a M.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering from UniTn, Trento. I grew up in the Veneto countryside, Italy.

🔭 I am interested in computational optimization and the interplay between numerical methods, linear algebra, and algorithms. My research activity comprises the design, analysis, and implementation of methods and codes for continuous optimization. Currently, I am working on stabilized, primal-dual Newton-type methods for (convex quadratic and) nonlinear programming problems.

🌱 I enjoy reading about the problem of consciousness and I am curious about how people came to be curious. Asimov's Foundation, War and Peace, and The Bridge on the Drina are among my favourite books. I am learning German and Julia, and I am looking forward to diving into Portuguese and Rust. When out of office, you can find me most often cooking or hiking.

📫 The best way to reach me is by e-mail.
